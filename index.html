About Us

The Farma Grains Ltd 'Mamacart' product, designed as a group food finance programme aims to empower small and medium sized food businesses through food supplies credit at a reasonable cost.

We provide primarily (but not exclusively) small and medium sized women owned food businesses access to food supplies credit on buy-now-pay-later basis.



Our promise to our customers is to delight them with

  • Fast and Easy Access to Food Supplies - Get food supplies without delays to help them remain in business.
  • Responsiveness - The product is designed to take care of the needs and preferences of the low income market, leveraging on their mutual trust and group collateral.
  • Excellent Customer Service - Our staff are friendly, competent and always willing to help. We also ensure there are no barriers matching client groups with dedicated customer service personnel.
  • Target Group Economically viable small and medium sized food businesses with potential to do daily business with capacity generating N100,000 - N1,000,000 per month.

Access Requirements

✓ Must be 18 - 60 years of age
✓ Must be in a group of 5 - 10 members who enjoy mutual trust, confidence and respect.
✓ The client must have an income generating food business which has been in existence for at least 6 months.
✓ The client must exhibit regular cash flow potential.
✓ Group members must know each other's physical location of business.
✓ Attend a 2 day online training before food supplies disbursement.
Collateral/Guarantees- Group Guarantee, Spouse's consent/other guarantor
Loan Term - Minimum period is 7 weeks
Grace period (Moratorium) - One week.
Repayment Frequency - Weekly.
Loan size - Minimum of N100, 000 to a maximum of N5,000,000 worth of food supplies.
Interest rate - 0% per month
Application and Registration fees - N15000
